 WHAT WE DO 
 Provider of leading edge high performance computing 
 
 Today many aspects of research, development and production, require substantial compute resources for simulations and data analysis.
 NSC provides HPC, data services and e&#8209;infrastructure competence for Swedish academic research, international collaborations, and our partners.
 We operate hardware resources and provide day&#8209;to&#8209;day as well as advanced user support for compute, storage, and analysis.
